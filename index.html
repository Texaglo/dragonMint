About Dragons:Origins

“Dragons:Origins“ is a collection of 10.000 unique, randomly generated characters from more then 80 hand-drawn traits. Each Dragon has levels of rarity that are based on trait percentage. The lower percentage the trait has, the rarer it is. Owning a Dragon will give you a variety of utilities and a chance to be a part of an amazing community. You can read more in our blogpost or discord (coming soon).
